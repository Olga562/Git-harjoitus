Tour Presentation
Day 1
Day 1

Welcome to Bali! The moment you step off the plane, you'll be greeted by our dedicated organizer, ready to assist you throughout your journey. We'll swiftly transfer you to your hotel, where you can settle in and take a moment to relax after your flight. As you unwind, you'll have the opportunity to meet and connect with the other participants of the tour, sharing in the excitement and anticipation for the adventures that await us.

Day 2
  • Full-day exploration of Ubud, the cultural heart of Bali
  • Visit the Sacred Monkey Forest Sanctuary and interact with playful macaques
  • Explore the traditional art market and discover unique handicrafts
  • Visit the Ubud Royal Palace and learn about its historical significance
  • Participate in a Balinese cooking class to learn traditional recipes and techniques
  • Return to the hotel for overnight stay

Day 3
  • Journey to the eastern part of Bali and immerse yourself in the captivating beauty and cultural wonders of this region.
  • Begin your day with a visit to Lempuyang Temple, often referred to as the "Gateway to Heaven.
  • Continue your journey to the Tirta Gangga Water Palace, a stunning royal water garden built by the Karangasem royal family.
  • Afterward, make your way to Taman Ujung, also known as the "Water Palace." This majestic complex showcases a harmonious blend of Balinese and European architectural influences.
  • As the day comes to a close, you'll return to your hotel with a newfound appreciation for Bali's cultural heritage and the breathtaking landscapes that make this island so unique.
Day 4
  • Rise early for an exhilarating trek to the summit of Mount Batur.
  • Witness the breathtaking sunrise over the island and enjoy panoramic views of the surrounding volcanic landscapes.
  • After the descent, visit a traditional coffee plantation and soak in natural hot springs to relax your muscles.

Day 5
  • Free Day in Bali
  • Enjoy a leisure day to relax at your hotel, explore the local area, or indulge in optional activities such as spa treatments or water sports.
  • Take this opportunity to immerse yourself in Bali's unique charm at your own pace.
Day 6
  • Depart Bali and travel by ferry to Java island.
  • Arrive in Banyuwangi and transfer to your hotel near Mount Ijen, where you will spend the night in preparation for the Ijen Blue Fire Tour.
  • Experience the warm hospitality and authentic flavors of Java by joining a traditional Javanese family for a memorable dinner.

Day 7
  • Start early for a memorable trek to the Ijen Crater, famous for its mesmerizing blue flames.
  • During your visit to Ijen, you'll have the opportunity to witness the remarkable determination and resilience of sulfur miners.
  • As the sun begins to rise, witness the transformation of the Ijen Crater. The turquoise-colored acidic lake takes center stage, surrounded by rugged volcanic walls.
  • After the Ijen adventure, transfer to the port and prepare for the next leg of your journey. Enjoy a scenic drive through Java's picturesque landscapes as you make your way to the port.
  • Board a speedboat that will whisk you away to the tranquil island of Gili Meno.
  • Spend the rest of the day at your leisure, unwinding on the beach, taking refreshing dips in the sea, or simply basking in the tropical ambiance of Gili Meno.
